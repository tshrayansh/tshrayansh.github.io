About Me

Hello ! I’m a third-year BS-MS student with academic interests in molecular biology, neurogenetics, and computational biology. Over the past few semesters, I’ve built a strong foundation in core biology subjects , alongside hands-on training in important lab techniques.

Beyond the lab, I actively explore the role of computational tools in biology. I’ve gained experience in Python, R, and C/C++, and I enjoy using programming for data analysis, automation, and scientific visualization. I’m also involved in student-led technical and academic initiatives — from helping manage coding clubs to organizing events that foster peer learning and research awareness.

I’m currently focused on deepening my experimental and analytical skills. I'm particularly interested in how genetic and molecular mechanisms influence neural development and function, and I hope to pursue further opportunities in this space through research internships and collaborative projects.
